About Us

ShivRav Logistics is a dynamic and devoted last-mile delivery company based in the heart of Jamshedpur, Jharkhand. Founded with the core principle of speed, safety, and sincerity, our mission is to simplify delivery for individuals, small businesses, and e-commerce brands across the region. Inspired by the strength and discipline of Lord Shiva and the entrepreneurial vision of our founder, Gaurav Jha, ShivRav Logistics is more than a company – it's a promise to deliver trust with every parcel.

At a time when fast and reliable delivery solutions have become the backbone of commerce, we saw a gap in local logistics services that catered to hyper-local, door-to-door needs while maintaining personal care and responsibility. ShivRav Logistics was born to fill that gap. With our strong local presence and fast-growing network, we ensure same-day and next-day delivery across Jamshedpur and nearby regions. From personal parcels to business documents, from COD (Cash-on-Delivery) items to sensitive medical or legal deliveries – we handle everything with care, accountability, and punctuality.

We understand that your parcel is not just a package – it's your trust, your business, your connection with someone. That's why we have made it our business to deliver more than goods – we deliver peace of mind.

Our team is powered by young, energetic logistics professionals who work with local knowledge, cutting-edge tools, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We don’t believe in one-size-fits-all. Whether you are a home-based seller, a small startup, a coaching center, or an individual needing something delivered quickly and safely, we offer flexible, affordable, and transparent logistics solutions for you.

Some of our popular services include:

  • Express Parcel Delivery within city limits (same day or 24-hour turnaround)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) support for online and local sellers
  • Secure Document Handling with doorstep pickup and delivery
  • B2C Last-Mile Delivery for Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho and other platforms
  • Bulk Pickup Services for shops, sellers, and businesses

What sets us apart is not just our punctuality or pricing, but the personal relationship we build with every client. We’re proud to be small enough to know our customers by name and committed enough to be available on call, WhatsApp, and even doorstep pickup without hassle.

As we grow, our aim is to expand into a smart delivery ecosystem powered by technology – with online tracking, automated scheduling, and a customer app for full control. But at our core, we will always remain a local, trusted partner who believes in serving the community with devotion and discipline.

At ShivRav Logistics, we deliver with a purpose – and that purpose is you. Let us help you move faster, smarter, and safer – whether it’s a simple envelope or a complex e-commerce shipment.

Fast. Flexible. Faithful – That’s ShivRav Logistics.
